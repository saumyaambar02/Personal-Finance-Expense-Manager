# statement.md

**Problem Statement**

Many individuals struggle to track their daily expenses and manage monthly budgets.
This project solves that problem by providing users with a simple and easy way to track
everyexpense,stay within their budget limits, and make better financial choices.

**Scope:**

The project focuses on:
Keeping a record of transactions with their date, amount, category, type, and optional notes
Providing clear summaries
Showing visual charts for better understanding
Allowing users to export their monthly reports for personal records


**Target Users:**

Students
Homemakers
Freelancers
Budget-conscious individuals
Adults
Young professionals


**High-level Features:**

Easily create, update, and delete transactions.
Get monthly summaries of income, expenses, and savings.
Receive budget alerts whenever spending exceeds the set limit.
View visualization charts for better understanding.
